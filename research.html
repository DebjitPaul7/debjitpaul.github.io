My research investigates the dynamics of atmospheric convection, the Indian monsoon system, and their variability under changing climatic conditions. I use numerical models, reanalysis datasets, and satellite observations to study mesoscale convective systems and their role in rainfall extremes.

Recognition

  • AGU Precipitation Technical Committee – Student Award (2024), Top 5 presentations globally.
  • First international recognition for research on South Asian Mesoscale Convective Systems.
  • Presented award-winning work at AGU Fall Meeting, one of the largest Earth science conferences.
  • Acknowledged for high-impact contribution to understanding MCS-driven rainfall extremes in South Asia.
  • Research spotlight featured in Times Square, New York during AGU (ECSPrecip showcase).
