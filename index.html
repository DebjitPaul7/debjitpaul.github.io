About Me

Welcome! I am a research scholar at the Centre for Atmospheric Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My research focuses on atmospheric convection, monsoon dynamics, and climate variability. I aim to better understand the physical processes driving extreme weather and rainfall variability across South Asia.
