About Me

I am a research scholar at the Centre for Atmospheric Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My research focuses on atmospheric convection, monsoon dynamics, and climate variability. I aim to improve our understanding of extreme weather systems and their interactions with large-scale climate phenomena.
